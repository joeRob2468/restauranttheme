Our Location

The Laughing Place Bakery is a small bakery that is passionate about good flavors. Our heart is to bring back a taste of the past through flavors. Apple pie like your grandmother baked...fresh baked bread and cookies like your mama made them...this is a place where community, fellowship, and laughter goes hand in hand. Come on in for a drink and some goodies, and stay awhile for a visit!

We are located at 504 NE 70th St B, Gladstone, MO 64118
